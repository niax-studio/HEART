Symptoms of Heart Disease

Recognizing symptoms early can save lives. Watch for:

  • Chest pain or discomfort
  • Shortness of breath
  • Lightheadedness or dizziness
  • Fatigue
  • Swelling in the legs or feet
  • Irregular heartbeat
